
# Hospitality Revenue Analysis Dashboard – Atliq Grand

## Overview

This project contains a revenue analysis dashboard created using Power BI for Atliq Grand, a chain of five-star hotels in India. The objective was to analyze historical data and generate meaningful insights to help the company regain lost market share and improve business performance.

## Problem Statement

Atliq Grand has been operating in the Indian hospitality industry for over two decades. Recently, the company has seen a decline in revenue and market share due to strong competitor strategies and inefficient decision-making. The management decided to invest in Business and Data Intelligence but lacked an internal analytics team. As a result, they outsourced the project to an external analyst.

## Objective

- Build KPIs and metrics based on stakeholder requirements.
- Design a Power BI dashboard according to the provided mock-up.
- Deliver additional business insights from the data.

## Tools Used

- Power BI (for dashboard creation)
- DAX (for calculated measures and KPIs)
- Excel/CSV (for raw data)

## Key Metrics Developed

- Revenue
- RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)
- DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights)
- ADR (Average Daily Rate)
- Occupancy %
- Realization %
- Cancellation %
- Average Rating

## Key Insights

- Mumbai generated the highest revenue (₹660M+), followed by Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Delhi.
- Atliq Exotica (Mumbai) recorded the highest revenue at ₹117M and the best average rating of 4.32.
- Atliq Palace Delhi had the highest occupancy rate at 66.25%.
- May was the month with the highest total revenue (₹581.9M).
- Elite room type generated the most revenue (₹553.7M).
- Atliq City Bangalore had the highest cancellation rate (26.46%).
- Weekdays had the most bookings (~69%).
- Direct online and travel agency platforms had the highest realization percentages.
- Revenue contribution was nearly equal from both luxury and business categories.
